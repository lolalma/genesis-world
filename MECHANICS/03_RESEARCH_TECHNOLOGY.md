# Research and Technology Trees Documentation

## Overview
This document provides a comprehensive overview of the research and technology trees across all eras in the Genesis World, detailing the various technology categories, their prerequisites, and progression systems.

## Eras and Technology Trees

### Era 1: Prehistoric
- **Technology Tree:** 
  - Fire
  - Basic Tools
  - Hunting Techniques
  
### Era 2: Ancient
- **Technology Tree:** 
  - Agriculture
  - Pottery 
  - Animal Domestication
  
### Era 3: Classical
- **Technology Tree:** 
  - Advanced Agriculture
  - Metallurgy
  - Writing
  
### Era 4: Medieval
- **Technology Tree:** 
  - Guild Systems
  - Navigation
  - Feudal Systems
  
### Era 5: Industrial
- **Technology Tree:** 
  - Steam Power
  - Manufacturing
  - Mass Production
  
### Era 6: Modern
- **Technology Tree:** 
  - Electronics
  - Information Technology
  - Biotechnology

## Progression Systems
Each technology requires specific prerequisite technologies to unlock further advancements. Players must strategize their research choices to optimize their progression through the ages.

### Tips for Players
- Focus on early technologies that enhance resource gathering 
- Prioritize technologies that unlock new units and buildings
- Consider long-term benefits of certain technologies over immediate gains

## Conclusion
Understanding these trees is essential for maximizing your strategy and success in the Genesis World. Plan your technology path wisely!
