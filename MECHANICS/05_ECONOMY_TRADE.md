# Economy and Trade Documentation

## Currency Systems

In the Genesis World, there are multiple currency systems that govern trade and commerce. The primary currency is **Gold Coins**, which are used for general transactions. Other currencies include **Silver Tokens** for specialized trades and **Trade Credits** for inter-city exchanges.

### Gold Coins
- **Value:** Basic unit of currency for everyday transactions.
- **Acquisition:** Can be earned through quests, selling goods, or finding treasures.

### Silver Tokens
- **Value:** 1 Silver Token = 10 Gold Coins.
- **Usage:** Primarily used in specific markets and trade fairs.

### Trade Credits
- **Value:** Digital currency used for large transactions between cities.
- **Usage:** Facilitates commerce between different economic zones in the world.

## Trading Mechanics

Trading in Genesis World involves several mechanics:
- **Direct Trading:** Players can trade items and currencies directly with one another.
- **Auction Houses:** Items can be auctioned to the highest bidder.
- **Marketplaces:** Central hubs where players can buy and sell items using the various currencies.

## Supply Chains

The supply chain in Genesis World is crucial for ensuring that goods are produced, transported, and sold effectively. Key components include:
- **Production:** Guilds and factories produce goods based on resource availability.
- **Transport:** Merchants use different means (caravans, ships, etc.) to transport goods to marketplaces.
- **Sales:** Goods are sold at varying prices depending on demand and rarity.

## Merchant Systems

Merchants play a vital role in the economy:
- **NPC Merchants:** Non-player characters that offer a set variety of goods.
- **Player Merchants:** Players can set up their own shops and determine pricing based on the economy.
- **Guild Merchants:** Specialized merchants that offer unique items related to specific guilds.

## Market Dynamics

Market dynamics are influenced by several factors:
- **Supply and Demand:** Prices fluctuate based on the availability of goods and player demand.
- **Events:** Seasonal events or quests can impact prices and availability of goods.
- **Inflation:** Over time, the introduction of new currencies or items can cause inflation affecting trade.

## Commerce

Commerce in Genesis World is not just about exchanging goods; it encompasses the entire economic system:
- **Trade Agreements:** Players and guilds can form alliances for better trade deals.
- **Economic Zones:** Different areas of the world have unique economic traits, fostering specialized goods.
- **Resource Management:** Players must manage resources wisely to thrive economically.
