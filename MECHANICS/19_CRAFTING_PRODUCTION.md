# Crafting and Production Documentation

## Crafting Systems
Crafting systems allow players to create items and equipment by combining resources. This process often involves specific recipes that dictate what items are needed to produce a desired outcome.

### Types of Crafting Systems
- **Basic Crafting**: Combine basic resources to create simple items.
- **Advanced Crafting**: Merge complex items or use special resources to create higher-tier equipment.

## Item Creation

### Recipes
Each crafted item is defined by a recipe that details:
- Required resources
- Tool or machines necessary for crafting
- Time and skill levels needed for crafting

## Production Chains
Production chains are sequences of crafting processes that transform raw resources into final products.

### Key Components
1. **Raw Materials**: The base resources needed for crafting.
2. **Intermediate Products**: Items created during the crafting process that will undergo further refinement.
3. **Final Products**: The ultimate items produced and ready for use or sale.

## Manufacturing
Manufacturing refers to the methods and processes used to produce goods. This may include:
- Automated production lines
- Manual crafting stations
- Quality control checks to ensure product fidelity

## Resource Processing
This involves transforming raw materials into usable resources before they can be crafted. Examples include:
- Smelting ores into metal bars
- Refining crude materials into usable components.

### Processing Techniques
- **Smelting**: Heating materials to extract metals from ores.
- **Milling**: Grinding substances to extract valuable components.
