# Magic and Supernatural Systems Documentation

## Magic Systems
### Types of Magic
1. **Elemental Magic**: Harnessing the power of nature, practitioners manipulate earth, water, fire, and air.
2. **Arcane Magic**: Focused on the manipulation of raw magical energies; often requires deep study and understanding.
3. **Divine Magic**: Granted by deities, used by clerics and paladins to heal, protect, and smite foes.
4. **Necromancy**: The art of communicating with the dead or raising the undead.
5. **Illusion Magic**: Creates false images or sounds, deceiving the senses.

## Spells
### Example Spells
- **Fireball**: A powerful projectile of flame; deals area damage.
- **Healing Light**: Restores health to the target.
- **Invisibility**: Renders the caster unseen for a short duration.

## Rituals
### Common Rituals
- **Ritual of Summoning**: Used to call forth magical entities.
- **Ritual of Binding**: Anchors a supernatural being to a physical object.
- **Ritual of Transmutation**: Alters the properties of materials.

## Supernatural Events
### Notable Events
1. **The Great Convergence**: A rare alignment of magical ley lines that enhances spell potency.
2. **The Night of Shadows**: A night where supernatural occurrences increase dramatically.
3. **The Awakening**: A period where ancient beings return to the realm.

## Magical Mechanics
### Key Mechanics
- **Mana System**: Energy source for spellcasting; replenishes over time or through specific actions.
- **Spell Slots**: Limitations on the number of spells a caster can use before resting.
- **Ritual Components**: Items or ingredients required to perform certain rituals.
