# Disease and Health Systems Documentation

## Disease Types
1. **Infectious Diseases**  
   Examples: Viral infections, bacterial infections.
2. **Chronic Diseases**  
   Examples: Heart disease, diabetes.
3. **Genetic Disorders**  
   Examples: Cystic fibrosis, sickle-cell anemia.
4. **Mental Health Disorders**  
   Examples: Depression, anxiety.

## Epidemiology
- Study of how diseases spread and how they can be controlled.
- Key factors include:
  - Transmission pathways  
  - At-risk populations  
  - Environmental influences  

## Medicine Research
- Focus on:
  - Drug development
  - Vaccine research
  - Clinical trials
- Importance of ethical standards in research.

## Healing Mechanics
1. **Natural Healing**  
   - Body's intrinsic ability to heal.
2. **Medical Interventions**  
   - Use of medications such as antibiotics or antivirals.
3. **Surgical Procedures**  
   - Necessary for conditions that cannot be treated with medicine alone.

## Public Health
- Promotion of health through:
  - Disease prevention programs
  - Health education
  - Community health initiatives  
- Importance of vaccinations and hygiene education.
